Sentimental Items Guide

The following categories of items are flagged for priority recovery when found in a storage unit. These items hold personal and irreplaceable value beyond their market worth. Cut The Lock sets these aside before any items go to our resale marketplace. If you believe an item from your unit falls into any of these categories, please let us know — we're committed to reuniting you with the things that matter most.

📸
Photos & Memories
  • Photo albums
  • Loose photographs
  • Negatives / film rolls
  • Home videos (VHS, Hi8, MiniDV, DVD)
  • Scrapbooks
  • Baby books
  • Wedding albums / guest books
  • Yearbooks
📝
Personal Documents
  • Birth / death / marriage certificates
  • Adoption papers
  • Passports
  • Immigration documents
  • Military service records (DD-214)
  • Diplomas / degrees
  • Medical records
  • Family Bibles with genealogy
  • Personal journals / diaries
  • Handwritten letters / cards
  • Children's report cards
🏅
Awards & Service
  • Military medals / dog tags / uniforms / flags
  • Trophies / plaques (personal)
  • Eagle Scout / Girl Scout awards
  • Varsity letters
  • First responder badges
  • Retirement plaques
⚱️
Remains & Religious
  • Cremated remains / urns
  • Religious texts with personal inscriptions
  • Rosaries / prayer beads / devotional items
  • Baptism / communion / confirmation keepsakes
  • Memorial items (funeral programs, obituaries)
🧸
Children's Keepsakes
  • Children's artwork / school projects
  • Baby's first shoes / clothing / blankets
  • Handprint / footprint casts
  • Tooth fairy boxes / first haircut keepsakes
  • Childhood stuffed animals
  • Growth charts
💍
Personal Heirlooms
  • Family jewelry with inscriptions / engravings
  • Heirloom furniture (handmade cradles, etc.)
  • Handmade quilts / blankets
  • Cultural / heritage items
  • Engraved watches / lockets with photos
🎨
Personal Creations
  • Original artwork by owner / family
  • Musical compositions / recordings
  • Poetry / unpublished writing
  • Craft projects
  • Handmade woodworking items
🐾
Pet Memorabilia
  • Pet ashes / urns
  • Paw print casts
  • Pet collars / tags (deceased pets)
📦
Irreplaceable Items
  • Custom family portraits
  • Time capsules
  • Collections with personal documentation
  • Items with personal engravings / dedications
💡
Our Guideline
If an item has a a name, date, or personal inscription — we consider it sentimental. These items are never sold. If you've lost something that matters to you, reach out — there's a good chance we have it. Cut The Lock: Recover What Matters Most.
